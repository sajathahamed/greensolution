Why Termite Control Matters

Subterranean termites are among the most destructive structural pests in the world. They work silently, often undetected, and can cause serious damage to wooden structures, foundations, and furniture.

At Green Solution Pest Management, we use proven anti-termite treatments and preventive measures to protect your home and business across Batticaloa, Trincomalee, and Ampara. Early inspection and treatment can save you costly repairs.
